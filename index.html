Fyodor Dostoevsky main info

Fyodor Dostoevsky from Bungo Stray Dogs is an enigmatic character known for his complex personality and deep philosophical insights. He is known for his mysterious past and saviour complex.

Fyodor is the head of a global criminal organization called the Rats in the House of the Dead. Major characters known to be a part of this organization include Ivan Goncharov, Nikolai Gogol, and Alexander Pushkin. Beside Fyodor himself, of course.

Fyodor is a master manipulator, often using his intelligence and charisma to influence others to do his bidding. He is also known for his love of literature and philosophy, often quoting famous works and engaging in deep discussions about the nature of existence.

In the series, he doesn't show up much before the final chapters. Although he is the antagonist of the series. After all, he almost never does the dirty work himself. And when he does, it isn't shown as much.

Personality

Fyodor is a complex person with a multifaced personality. He might seem emotionless and uncaring at first sight. But in truth, he isn't has heartless as he seems to be at first glance. This is because he is searching for salvation for others. He believes he has been given the diven order from God himself to purge the world of sins. And so he takes on the burden of this self given task.

He is extremely intelligent, intelligent to the point that it even rivals Ranpo Edogawa and Osamu Dazai's intelligence. But he is also extremely frail. He has been noted to have anemia in the series, and avoids physical exertion.

Fyodor enjoys sweets, especially cakes and other pastries. He is also seen to enjoy tea. Other of his likes include his ushanka hat, playing the cello, standing on high rooftops, and people with "good complexions".

Although Fyodor may seem to appear as a very genteel and polite person, he is deeply manipulative. He hides behind masks and controls others easily as if they were mere puppets without them even noticing. At least, usually, this is the case. Although not every time, such as seen with Nikolai and Sigma who seem to be extremely aware of that they are being manipulated and used by Fyodor.

Fyodor's only currently known dislike is humanity's sinful nature. This is deeply linked with his wish to "bring salvation" to others. The thing we know for certain he views as sinful is abilities. This might, or might not, be related to a traumatic past. But since nothing from his past is known, nothing can be said for certain.

Appearance

Fyodor's appearance changes a bit throughout the series and his life due to his ability Crime and Punishment, which allows him to take over his killer's body. And therefore he gets the age, height, and weight of that person.

But he is usually seen wearing a black coat with a white dress shirt and matching white pants. The shirt has purple buttons and the collar is also distinctively purple. Alongside this he wears a white ushanka hat which seems to be quite beloved to him. For shoes he wears maroon boots.

Fyodor has medium long messy black hair and deep purple eyes. His skin is extremely pale charactarized by his anemia. This makes him look quite frail and vulnerable. Although he is sickly looking, he does look refined and polished. We see a lot of the fanart and official art depict him as an attractive individual.

Fyodor is a slender and slim man, and looks to be in his late twenties to early thirties. Although his actual age is still unknown to everyone.

Ability and skills

Fyodor Dostoevsky's ability's true nature is still unknown, but some of it is known. Apparently, his touch can kill, but that is also unsure for now. But what IS known about his ability is to take over the body of his killer and make thim appear like him.

A popular theory of the touch part of his ability would be that his touch is deadly to those who feel guilty or have sinned. And while the theory has some flaws... Who knows? Maybe it actually is true, only that Fyodor can control it well.

Another characteristic of his ability is that as he is taking over the body of his killer, he gains the ability to live for centuries. This is seen as Sigma sees into Fyodor's memories and notes how Fyodor lived at the same time as Bram was still a highly noble royalty. Which would mean he is over 500 years old.
